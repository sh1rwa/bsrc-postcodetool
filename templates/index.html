Bristol Postcode to Ward Lookup

How to use this tool

  • Enter one or more postcodes into the box below.
  • Separate postcodes using commas or new lines.
  • Click Lookup to find the corresponding ward.
  • Click suggestions to quickly correct any incorrect postcodes.
  • Invalid inputs will be highlighted and excluded from downloads.

    • Example:

    BS1 1AD
BS1 1BT, BS1 1BU
